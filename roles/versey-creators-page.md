## What you'd build

A use-case sub-page on `versey.ai` for **newsletter writers** — a positioning-led page that shows newsletter writers what Versey does for them specifically, with copy, visuals, examples, and a clear next action. Sits within the existing Astro marketing site (`apps/www`).

## The hard part

The hard part: a use-case page that newsletter writers self-identify with in the first scroll. Most "for creators" pages are either too generic (could apply to anyone) or too narrow (only fits one archetype). The bar is: a newsletter writer hits the page, sees an example that's recognisably *their* workflow — drafting an issue, repurposing past posts, building a backlog — and gets to the call-to-action without scrolling past anything that feels like it wasn't written for them.

The second hard part: copy first, design second. A pretty page with vague copy doesn't convert. The page lives or dies on the headline, the three example use-cases, and the CTA wording.
